Glidey is a smart, evolving e-assist kit for 12"-20" bikes. Featuring a Soft-Start algorithm for balance and a Parent App for remote speed control. With an aero-cooling shell, it breathes new life into old bikes.

To eliminate the frustration of learning to ride and completely erase parental safety anxieties, our team has created Glidey.

Carried by Glidey's smooth momentum, kids no longer panic to find their footing. Once they feel the magic of a stable glide, mastering the ride becomes beautifully intuitive.

Glidey's gentle push eliminates wobbling right from the start. Kids simply hold the handlebars and focus on their balance.

Designed for ages 4-12 with 3 unlocking modes: Balance Mode, Normal Assist, and Explorer Mode. One kit grows with them.

The Glidey Parent App acts as an invisible safety net, putting total control at your fingertips. From speed limiting to an instant kill-switch.

Glidey is perfectly compatible with your existing bike. Retain your riding experience and configuration. Compatible with city, mountain, road, and folding bikes.
